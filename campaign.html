Our Mission

Too many Canada's youths are scarred for life by sexualpredators; some touches never go away. Wolf Watch is a non-profit student run organisation that aims to lower the number of child sexual abuse victims. Through our latestsocial issue campaign introducing our online and phone hotline, parents’ guide,advertisements, and videos, we aim to raise awareness about child sexual abuseand support children at risk.

Who's at Risk?

  • 1 in 5 girls and 1 in 20 boys
  • 28% of U.S. youth ages 14 to 17
  • 3 out of 4 victims were assaulted by someone they knew well
  • women commit 14% to 40% of offenses reported against boys
