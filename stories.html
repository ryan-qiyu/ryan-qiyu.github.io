Children's Abuse Stories

Stories from the kids who've experienced it, and how we've helped them.

Margaret

Parent Victim

"To outsiders we were picture perfect, but behind closed doors life was very different. I felt trapped. As the children got older Tony started being abusive towards them too. He would touch and beat them with belts and shoes. He would punish them when I wasn’t in the house so I couldn’t intervene. The Wolf Watch's counseling with my children gave them the light back behind their eyes."

Margaret 42 Years Old

Lee

Child Victim

"I didn't have a lot of friends so I felt like I'd made a close friend in Adam. He was paying me attention and I enjoyed his company, it felt like he was really looking out for me. I felt helpless to tell anybody about what was happening. I was so confused but knew what he was doing was wrong. My mom called Wolf Watch and their advisors helped me get away as well as the right officials to call."

Lee 16 Years Old

Maisie

Child Victim

"We were getting called into her school more and more regularly as Maisie’s disruptive behaviour escalated. It was worrying because I had never seen Maisie behave in this way and she wasn’t displaying any of these behaviours at home. I noticed that after school holidays we would get called in more often than towards the end of term. I asked her if anyone had touched her anywhere private other than me or her father. As always, I expected her to say no but she didn't, she said yes. I found Wolf Watch online and thank god. Their helpline helped me communicate with my daughter and told us exactly who to call to keep her safe."

Maisie 4 Years Old
